About me

I'm a PhD student in EDA at James Watt School of Engineering, University of Glasgow, UK, under supervision of Prof. Bo Liu. I obtained a dual BEng degree in Electronic Engineering from University of Glasgow and UESTC and an MS in Computer Science from Georgia Institute of Technology. During Bachelor, I worked with Prof. Sajeewa doing Bioinformatics.

My research interests lie in Machine Learning and optimization algorithm, especially for Electronic Design Automation (EDA).
