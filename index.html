About me

I'm an incoming PhD student at James Watt School of Engineering, University of Glasgow, UK, under supervision of Prof. Bo Liu. I received BEng (Hons) in Electronic Engineering from University of Glasgow and UESTC and MSc in Computer Science (GPA 4.0/4.0) from Georgia Tech, US.
